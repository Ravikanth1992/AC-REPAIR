FAQ's [Frequently Asked Questions]:- About Us

  1. What services does Infinity AC Repair offer?

    We provide a full range of HVAC services including AC repair, installation, maintenance, heating repair, ductwork, thermostat services, and indoor air quality solutions.

  2. Are your technicians certified and insured?

    Absolutely. All of our technicians are fully licensed, certified, and insured to perform HVAC repairs and installations.

  3. How quickly can you schedule a repair appointment?

    We strive to offer same-day service whenever possible. Call us or book online to check current availability.

  4. Do you provide free estimates?

    Yes, we offer free estimates for most repair jobs. Diagnostics may involve a small fee, which is waived if you proceed with the repair.

  5. How often should I service my AC unit?

    We recommend scheduling maintenance at least once a year, ideally before the summer season begins.
